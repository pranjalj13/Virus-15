The ENIAC Club

The official computer club at Bluebells School International has been initiated and managed completely by us, the students. ENIAC officially means Electronic Numerical Integrator and Calculator, but to us it is much more than that. It means Encoding, Networking, Innovating, Achieving and Computing. Overall it is a high octane filled, adrenaline rush, powered by the brains of the members of the club. Our previous events, VIRUS 2001, 2003 and Enter-o-Virus 2005, 2006, 2007, 2008, and 2009 were a roaring success, thanks to all the participating teams.

VIRUS'15

Events

PROGRAMMING

Number of participants: 2
Software: Turbo C++ 4.5
Time Allowed: 120 Minutes
Eligibility: XI‐XII

You will be provided with a computer, and a set of four Tasks. There will be a 30 minutes objective type preliminary round to find the finalist among you. The prelims would consist of an MCQ, one word answer questions and an output finding round, participants are requested to bring HB pencil set with eraser.

MOVIE MAKING

Number of participants: 2
Eligibility: Open

The participants are requested to make a movie on the topics given below and present it to the judges on the day of the event. The movie should be of maximum 3 minutes.
Plagiarism will lead to disqualification.

The topics are:
1. Technology has loosened familial ties
2. Media has been responsible for increased crime rates against women
3. The new age woman believes in breaking stereotypes
4. Heritage preservation should be a national movement in India

QUIZ

Number of participants: 2

Eligibility: Open

The 30 minutes objective type prelims round will shortlist 6 teams for the final on‐stage round.

POWERPOINT

Number of Team: 1
Number of participants: 2
Time Allowed: 45 Minutes
Eligibility: Class IV and V

Participants will be asked to use Microsoft PowerPoint to design and prepare a presentation. The topic along with a few facts, images and Audio and Video files related to that topic will be given on the spot. No external material will be allowed.

RUBIK's CUBE

Number of participants: 3
Eligibility: Open
Maximum time per solve: 5 minutes (3x3x3) || 7 minutes (Pyraminx)

  • This event is speed-‐solving competition of the 3x3x3 Rubik's cube and Pyraminx. The event will consist of 2 rounds. The winners will be determined on the basis of their average time of the two rounds.
  • Competitors are requested to bring their own cubes. Cubes will not be provided on the spot. Cubes with 6 distinct colors and of size 3x3x3 and Pyraminx will only be permitted.
    • Note: The 3x3x3 competition and Pyraminx competitions are independent of each other.

    GAMING

    Number of participants: 2

    Eligibility: Open

    Type: Console Gaming

    Rules will be intimated to the participants on the spot. Participants must be prepared to face any kind of game.

    DIGITAL IMAGING

    Number of teams: 1
    Number of Participants: 2
    Time Allotted: 60 Minutes
    Software: Adobe Photoshop CS6
    Eligibility: Open

    This challenging event encompasses the skills required in the field of poster designing.

    Entries will be judged on the basis of originality, creativity, code, functionality and aesthetics.

    RULES

    1. To be eligible for the winning trophy the school must participate in all the events.

    2. In case of a tie for overall position the team which has positions in more number of events shall get the trophy or one which registered earlier.

    3. All participants and guests are requested to arrive at the venue by 8:00 a.m. sharp. Schools coming in late will not be given any extra time for the events they miss.

    4. Schools are requested to confirm the details of the participation and also register via Email latest by 17th November, 2015 at bbs.eniac@gmail.com or you can register on the ENIAC website: www.bbseniac.com
